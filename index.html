ART

Overview

Art has been around for longer than you. You see art everyday, whether you realize it or not. Art can be connected to everything from a drawing you drew to a design on your clothing. It can be connected to everything in humanity to innovations in technology! Read on to learn a little more about the BIG world of art and technology.

Art Innovations In The Past

Visual Art

Autodesk Maya

It is a software program used for animation, modeling, simulation and rendering by artists/scientists. This program makes computer graphics and animation fast and easy.

  • Autodesk Maya was one of the software programs used to make Frozen 2!
AARON

A first generation computer art program that was invented in 1973 used to create original artwork on a computer screen.

3D Pens/Printing

Gadgets that are used to make art to the next level creating sculptures and 3D designs.

  • The LIX pen is a 3D printing pen that can be used to create free standing structure in mid air!
SketchPad

The first interactive computer-graphics program/device that used a digital drawing screen and a light-pen to form art.

Commercial Art

Rarible

A trustworthy platform used to create, collect, or sell digital art (Non Fungible Token).

  • One of the most expensive (69.3 million dollars) NFTs ever sold goes to the "EVERYDAYS: THE FIRST 5000 DAYS" created by digital artist Mike "Beeple" Winkelmann

Innovations In The Future

Mind Art

A device that allows you to create whatever is on your mind without having to physically show it using paper

  • In the shape of a headset that can be connected to another technology (using Bluetooth)
  • Automatically saves your work
The Smart Fan

Abstract art made by a fan like machine

  • Abstract art made by a fan-like machine
  • Sprays/throws paint
  • Manipulate it using a small ipad
  • Chargeable (No need for battery replacement)
The FireFli

A program that creates accurate faces/objects (representational art) based of verbal descriptions

  • Easily manageable (Draw and erase many times)
  • Automatically saves art
  • Can also be used in other fields (Ex: A witness can describe a criminal and FireFli will sketch them out)
iART

A game-like program that’s based on art

  • Include an endless amount of levels
  • Uses a joypad (Multiplayer/Single Player)
  • Develops people’s artistic skills
